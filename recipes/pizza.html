Pizza

Description

Pizza is an Italian dish but not really. Some people say that it is proof that God loves us.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup all purpose flour.
  • 100 gm processed cheese.
  • 100 ml tomato ketchup.
  • 4 mushroom.
  • 1 tomato.
  • 1/2 capsicum (green pepper)
  • 2 onion.
  • 1 teaspoon oregano.

Steps

  1. Start with a solid pizza dough recipe.
  2. Make the dough.
  3. Proof the dough.
  4. Prepare the sauce and toppings.
  5. Shape the dough.
  6. Bake the pizza.
  7. Cool the pizza.
