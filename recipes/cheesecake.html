Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is heavenlike. Like hot didgeridoo, I love this one.

Ingredients

  • four 8-ounce blocks (904g) full-fat cream cheese, softened to room temperature.
  • (200g) granulated sugar.
  • 1 cup (240g) full-fat sour cream, at room temperature.
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract.
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice (optional, but recommended)
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature.

Steps

  1. Step 1: Use a Springform Pan.
  2. Step 2: Let Ingredients Stand.
  3. Step 3: Press Crust Mixture into Pan.
  4. Step 4: Prepare and Beat the Filling.
  5. Step 5: Gently Stir in Eggs.
  6. Step 6: Pour Filling into Pan.
  7. Step 7: Set Inside a Larger Pan and Bake.
  8. Step 8: Check Cheesecake for Doneness.
